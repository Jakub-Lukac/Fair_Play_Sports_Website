FLOORBALL CAMP 2024

11th year of FLOORBALL CAMP FAIR PLAY SPORTS 2024 in partnership with Fakultou telesnej výchovy, športu a zdravia UMB in Banská Bystrica. Date : 1st – 5th of July 2024 daily from 8:00 – 16:00 hod. In hall of Fakulty telesnej výchovy, športu a zdravia UMB, Tajovského 40, Banská Bystrica and hall Školská 7, Banská Bystrica. We offer a daily floorball camp for beginners and advanced players in the range of 5 days for your children to spend their time meaningfully during the summer holidays. It is primarily intended for children from the 1st to the 9th grade of elementary school. For more info.
