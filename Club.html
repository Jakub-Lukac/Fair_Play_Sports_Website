About us

Civic association Fair play sports actively ascts since 2011. Our goal is the support of healthy lifestyle through regular movement activity – florbal. We organise regular sport events, competitions, training for kids, youth, adults.

Regular activities of the OZ FPS includes mostly floorball trainings for students of primary schools, Highschool floorball league, Floorball for public on recreational level, mostly for adults and National 1st floorball men's league.

You can view our results on our social media profiles, official website szfb and news of regional press town of Basnká Bystrica.
